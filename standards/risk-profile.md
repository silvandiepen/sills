# Risk profile

Sills exposes risk in two forms:

1. `risk-profile.json`, a machine-readable set of evidence-linked dimensions on a 0–5 ordinal scale;
2. a compact text visualization generated from the same data for Markdown and terminal reports.

The graph is not a product score. Every level must reference finding IDs and explain the dominant risk. Missing evidence is represented through coverage and confidence rather than guessed as low risk.
