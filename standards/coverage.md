# Audit coverage quality

Coverage reports what was tested, not a product-quality score. Each dimension records applicable and tested surfaces, roles, environments, and material limitations.

Percentages may be derived only when the denominator is known and meaningful. Otherwise use categorical coverage: complete, substantial, partial, minimal, not-tested, or not-applicable.

A conclusion must not exceed its evidence. Missing authenticated roles, runtime targets, native platforms, or critical user journeys can force `insufficient` coverage even when automated checks pass.
